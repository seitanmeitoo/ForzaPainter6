# Third-party notices

The `shapegen/` package in this repository is a port of the shape-generation
engine from **Forza-Painter** (MIT, 2026) — https://github.com/forza-painter/forza-painter — adapted to
the constraints of the Forza Horizon 6 vinyl format (only axis-aligned
rectangles and rotated ellipses are emitted).

Forza-Painter itself is inspired by and credits the techniques of:

- **forza-painter** by the_adawg (MIT) — https://github.com/forza-painter/forza-painter
- **geometrize-lib** by Sam Twidale (MIT) — https://samcodes.co.uk/
- **Primitive** by Michael Fogleman (MIT) — https://github.com/fogleman/primitive
